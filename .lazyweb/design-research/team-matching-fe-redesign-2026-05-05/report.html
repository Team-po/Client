Team-po FE Redesign Research

Date: 2026-05-05

TL;DR

The strongest references pointed toward a calm, operations-first app: compact navigation, status and next-action panels first, then progressively revealed details.

LazyWeb Findings

Reference Useful pattern
Userlane KPI cards, stage progress, and decision-first dashboard panels.
Are.na Workspace home with recent items and shared team collections.
Codecademy / Opal Step-based questionnaire and preference selection for matching.
Userguiding / Chameleon Member cards, status badges, and team management controls.
Rippling / Appcues Grouped profile actions and onboarding checklist panels.
Glean / Timely Activity timelines with project progress and status controls.

Principles

  • Decision-first dashboard hierarchy.
  • 3 to 5 primary signals per screen.
  • Progressive disclosure for detail-heavy team operations.
  • Existing blue/indigo primary colors, with functional status colors.
  • Auth flow treated as product onboarding.
